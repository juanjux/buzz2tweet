Who is after buzz2tweet.com?

Juanjo Alvarez, just a random programmer with the goal of playing a little with ActiveMQ and the STUMP protocol.

What personal data do you store? Do you sell the data or something?

The system only stores the (public) Google Profile URL and the Twitter (public) username. You (public) Buzzs contents will only be stored until they're published on Twitter. I wont give the data to any third party.

Can the service publish my tweets on Buzz?

Not yet because the Buzz API currently doesn't support publishing on Buzz. I'll update the service as soon as it does.

How much time until my buzzs appear on Twitter?

The Google Buzz's Atom feed usually updates withing 1-3 minutes after the publication of the Buzz, then we've to read and publish, but that's usually pretty fast (seconds.)

Why are some of my Buzzs not showing on Twitter?

It could be one of several things:

  1. Private buzzs: buzz2tweet doesn't publish private buzzs, only the public ones.
  2. Twitter is down: even if you can log into Twitter sometimes the apps using the API doesn't.
  3. buzz2tweet overload: since the service is new I don't know yet how it will handle the load if a lot of people starts using it. In theory it can scale pretty well... in theory.
